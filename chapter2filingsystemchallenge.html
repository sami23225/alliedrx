Key Record Management Principles:

  • Filing Systems: Choose appropriate system (alphabetic, numeric, terminal digit) based on practice size
  • Indexing Rules: Consistent application ensures efficient record retrieval
  • Record Classification: Proper classification determines storage location and retention period
  • Cross-Referencing: Essential for name changes and alternate spellings
Clinical Insight: In medical practice, efficient record management directly impacts patient care. A misfiled record can delay treatment, cause medication errors, or lead to HIPAA violations. Always double-check filing decisions and maintain meticulous cross-reference systems.
