Clinical Application

Clinical Assessment & Women's Health

Week 6 combines fundamental clinical assessment skills with comprehensive women's healthcare knowledge. You'll learn to accurately measure vital signs while developing expertise in obstetric and gynecologic care across the lifespan.

These chapters connect because accurate vital signs assessment is critical in both general practice and specialty OB/GYN care. A pregnant patient requires meticulous vital signs monitoring, while gynecologic patients need sensitive care during intimate examinations.
