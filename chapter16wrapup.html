Step 4

Chapter 16 Vocabulary

Albuminuria
Abnormal presence of albumin protein in urine; indicates kidney damage
Azotemia
Retention of nitrogenous wastes in blood; sign of renal impairment
Dysuria
Painful or difficult urination; common in UTIs and STIs
Creatinine
Nitrogenous waste from muscle metabolism; measured to assess kidney function
Urgency
Sudden compelling desire to urinate with inability to control release
Erythropoietin
Kidney-produced hormone that stimulates red blood cell production
Renin
Enzyme from kidneys that helps regulate blood pressure
Casts
Fibrous/protein material in urine shaped like kidney tubules; indicates kidney disease
