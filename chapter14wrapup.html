Step 2

Dermatology Vocabulary & Classification

Tap each card to reveal the definition.

Primary Lesions
Initial lesions: Macule, Papule, Vesicle, Bulla, Pustule, Nodule, Tumor, Wheal
Secondary Lesions
Evolve from primary: Scale, Crust, Erosion, Ulcer, Fissure, Scar, Atrophy
ABCDE Rule
Asymmetry, Border irregularity, Color variation, Diameter >6mm, Evolving
Punch Biopsy
Removes full-thickness skin sample using circular blade; requires suture
Dermatoscope
Handheld device with magnification and polarized light for skin examination
Mohs Surgery
Layer-by-layer excision with immediate microscopic examination for skin cancers

Lesion Classification:

Macule (Primary)
Papule (Primary)
Scale (Secondary)
Ulcer (Secondary)
Clinical Pearl: Any lesion with "E" (Evolving) in the ABCDE rule warrants referral regardless of other findings.
