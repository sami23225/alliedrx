Step 2

Infection Control Vocabulary

Tap each card to reveal the definition.

Chain of Infection
Sequence: Infectious agent → Reservoir → Portal of exit → Mode of transmission → Portal of entry → Susceptible host
Medical Asepsis
Clean technique that reduces pathogens and prevents spread (hand hygiene, disinfecting surfaces)
Standard Precautions
Infection prevention practices applied to all patients regardless of diagnosis
PPE
Personal Protective Equipment (gloves, gowns, masks, eye protection) worn to prevent exposure
Hand Hygiene
Washing hands with soap/water or using alcohol-based hand rub to remove pathogens
Transmission-Based Precautions
Additional precautions for specific infections: Contact, Droplet, Airborne
