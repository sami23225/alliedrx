What we're covering this week

Big picture

This week’s lesson focuses on competency-based education and how it shapes your learning experience at AlliedRx. Instead of only counting hours or seat time, competency-based education emphasizes the skills you can demonstrate in real clinical settings.

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Recognize how your program is structured around clearly defined competencies.
  • Connect your coursework, labs, and portfolio artifacts to your future role as a medical assistant.
  • Identify strategies to study more effectively based on your learning preferences.
Pro tip: Keep a running list of key terms, portfolio pieces, and “aha” moments from this week. You’ll refer back to these throughout the program and when you prepare for your NHA exam.
