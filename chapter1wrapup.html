Step 2

Key Vocabulary Flashcards

Select each card to reveal the definition. Can you explain each term in your own words without looking?

Competencies
The set of knowledge, hands-on skills, and professional behaviors you must demonstrate to practice safely as an entry-level medical assistant.
Critical Thinking
Looking at all sides of a situation, weighing the facts, and making a logical decision while setting aside personal bias.
Learning Style
Your preferred way of taking in and processing new information—for example, concrete vs. abstract and active vs. reflective learning.
Mnemonic
A memory aid—such as a phrase, picture, rhythm, or acronym—that helps you remember complex information.
Stressors & Coping
A stressor is any event or situation that creates stress. Adaptive coping mechanisms help you manage it; non-adaptive ones make it worse.
Reflection
Pausing to think about what you learned, how you learned it, and how it connects to your goals or patient care.
